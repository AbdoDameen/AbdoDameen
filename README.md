## 🧠 Profile

I build **data systems that solve operational problems**, with a strong focus on:

- 📦 Demand Forecasting & Inventory Planning  
- 📉 Reducing uncertainty in supply chains  
- ⚙️ Translating models into **real decision workflows**  

Over the past 3+ years, my work has centred on **appliance spare parts forecasting**, where demand is:
- Intermittent  
- Sparse  
- Driven by failure patterns rather than trends  

This is where most models fail — and where I focus.
